NeuroScan AI

AI-Powered Brain Tumor Detection and Classification using Convolutional Neural Networks

About the Project

NeuroScan AI is a brain tumor detection and classification system using CNNs to analyze brain MRI scans with high accuracy.

The goal is to automatically classify MRI scans as tumor or tumor-free, aiding early diagnosis and reducing radiologist workload.

Rapid, accurate preliminary assessments help clinicians focus on treatment planning while AI handles initial screening.
